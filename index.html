About Us

Building on two decades of the Venba Group, entering paints in 2026

Venba Shines Private Limited manufactures lead-free, eco-friendly architectural and decorative paints for local construction firms, hardware retailers and residential projects — meeting the rising demand for affordable, high-quality coatings in India's expanding real estate sector.

2005Venba Shrimps

The Venba Group's founding venture, establishing our commitment to quality and enterprise.

2010Venba Recoveries

Expansion into a second line of business, broadening the group's operational base.

2015Venba Organics

A move toward sustainable, organic-focused enterprise within the group.

2020Venba Horticulture

Continued diversification, deepening the group's expertise in sustainable production.

2026Venba Shines

The Venba Group enters paint manufacturing — protecting and beautifying India's homes.

Our Mission

To manufacture and distribute high-quality, durable coatings that protect and beautify surfaces.

Becoming an industry leader — innovating with eco-friendly products, expanding into global markets, and transforming living spaces.
